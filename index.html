Jiangming Liu (刘 江鸣)

I am a Ph.D. candidate in ILCC, School of Informatics, The University of Edinburgh. My advisors are Dr. Shay Cohen and Prof. Mirella Lapata.

Research interests are Semantic Parsing, Syntactic Parsing, Sentiment Analysis, Neural Machine Translation. The current research focus is Discourse Represntation Structure Parsing based on Discourse Representation Theory (DRT; Kamp, 1981), and its applications.
