Jiangming Liu (刘 江鸣)

I am a final-year PhD student in ILCC, School of Informatics, The University of Edinburgh.

My research interests are natural langauge processing and deep learning. I am working on semantic paring and text generation advised by Shay Cohen and Mirella Lapata. Last year, I worked with Matt Gardener on document comprehension QA as an intern in AI2. Before my PhD study, I was a research assistant in SUTD, working with Yue Zhang on syntactic parsing. I obtained my MSc degree on statistical machine translation in Beijing Jiaotong University advised by JinAn Xu.

I am on the job market!!!
